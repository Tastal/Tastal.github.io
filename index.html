About

I am an undergraduate student in Physics at the University of Waterloo, working as a research assistant under Dr. Barbara Zemskova in the Department of Applied Mathematics. My current research focuses on unsupervised learning for ocean-biogeochemical data, particularly the clustering of global dissolved inorganic carbon profiles using Gaussian Mixture Models.

I am broadly interested in the intersection of physics-based simulation and machine learning, aiming to develop real-time fluid dynamics models for interactive applications such as games and virtual environments.
